6.2 Machine Learning Validation & Reliability

Cross-Validation Analysis

To verify the generalizability of our model and ensure it is not overfitting, we conducted 5-Fold Stratified Cross-Validation on the LightGBM V2 model. Stratified folds were used to preserve class proportions (8.6% positive) in each fold.

Learning Curves

Learning curves were evaluated by tracking training and validation error against increasing dataset sizes (up to 319,795 rows):

  • The training and cross-validation scores converged smoothly at approximately 0.84 ROC-AUC.
  • The lack of gap between the two curves indicates that the model has **low variance** (no overfitting) and **low bias** (no underfitting).
